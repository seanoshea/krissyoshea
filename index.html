An artist, born in Grafton Massachusetts, Krissy Tosi received a BFA in both literature and political science at the University of Vermont in 2004, and completed a Post-Baccalaureat Certificate from the San Francisco Art Institute in 2008. Ms. Tosi was the curator for the Still Lights Gallery, San Francisco Art Institute (2009-2010) and has curated for the Diego Rivera Gallery, San Francisco Art Institute. Ms. Tosi has exhibited in "Born Into This" a group show at the Diego Rivera Gallery, San Francisco Art Institute (2009), as well as, the continuing "MFA show" at the Diego Rivera Gallery (2009), the Swell Gallery (2007), San Francisco Arts Institute and the Still Lights Gallery(2008), San Francisco Art Institute.
